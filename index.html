NHentai Downloader

A Chrome extension to easily download doujinshi from nhentai.net

Download from Chrome Web Store Source Code
(GitHub)

Download doujinshi directly from their page!

Just go on the page of any doujinshi, open the extension and download it.
Don't want to download them inside a ZIP file? You can choose your favorite format in the extension's option between zip, cbz and raw.

Download many doujinshi at a time

Go on any page containing doujinshi and download them all at once!
You can even use that to download the doujinshi in your favorites.

Download many pages at a time

Want to download all the doujinshi with some tags?
Go to any page with doujinshi and download all of them, along with the next pages.
